WHY WE GATHER

Because every child deserves a wish –
and every community has the power to help grant it!

Wish Upon a Food Truck Festival brings people together over great food, local flavor, and shared purpose. What starts as a fun day becomes something far greater: hope for children and families facing unimaginable challenges. Together, we turn meals into memories and generosity into wishes that truly change lives in support of Make-A-Wish Philadelphia, Delaware & Susquehanna Valley.
