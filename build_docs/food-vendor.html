FOOD TRUCK VENDOR
INFORMATION

Wish Upon a Food Truck Festival brings together an intentionally curated group of food trucks to create an unforgettable community experience – all while supporting Make-A-Wish and helping grant life-changing wishes for children and families in our region.

To ensure a diverse and successful festival, participation is limited to
12 food trucks total, with six savory and six sweet vendors selected.
All vendors must apply and submit payment through the online application below.
Submission does not guarantee acceptance.

FESTIVAL DETAILS:

  • Date: Saturday, September 12
  • Time: 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM
  • Location: AACA Museum, Hershey, PA
  • Set-Up: 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
    • All additional vehicles must be removed from festival grounds by 10:00 AM.
    • Food trucks must be fully operational and ready to serve by 10:00 AM.
    • Vendors may not leave prior to the event conclusion.

VENDOR SELECTION & GUIDELINES:

Our goal is to create a balanced, high-quality festival experience for attendees and vendors alike.

Selection is based on:

  • Completion of the online application and payment.
  • Menu offerings and product variety.
  • Balance between savory and sweet vendors.
  • Date of submission.

We reserve the right to limit the number of vendors offering similar menu items.

VENDOR FEE:

  • Food Truck Fee: $150 (Non-refundable upon acceptance).

If a vendor is not accepted, payment will be refunded.

WHAT VENDORS ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR:

  • Trucks must be fully self-sufficient (no electricity or water provided).
  • Quiet generators are permitted.
  • Vendors are responsible for maintaining a clean, professional setup.
  • Trash must be contained within your designated area.
  • Clear signage with visible pricing is required.
  • Vendors must remain open for the full duration of the festival.
  • All food trucks must have an on-site fire extinguisher serviced within the past year.

INSURANCE & LICENSING REQUIREMENTS:

All vendors are required to submit:

  • A valid food service license
  • Proof of vehicle insurance.
  • A Certificate of Insurance naming:
    • Make-A-Wish Philadelphia, Delaware & Susquehanna Valley
    • Wish Upon a Food Truck Festival Committee
    • Festival Volunteers
    • AACA Museum, Inc as additional insured

Certificates of Insurance must be submitted prior to the event.

MENU & BRANDING INFORMATION:

As part of the application, vendors will be asked to submit:

  • A photo of your truck/setup (used for promotional purposes)
  • Your logo (.png and .esp file formats)
  • A detailed menu list (maximum of 8 food items, excluding beverages)
  • Accepted payment methods.

WEATHER POLICY:

This is a rain-or-shine outdoor event. The festival will not be rescheduled due to the weather.
Vendor fees are non-refundable if the event is canceled due to severe weather or if you choose not to attend.

IMPORTANT PARTICIPATION NOTES:

  • All locations are assigned by festival organizers.
  • Only festival-approved tip jars supporting Make-A-Wish are permitted.
  • Vendors are responsible for securing all required local and state permits.
  • Participation requires agreement to the festival’s liability release and hold harmless provisions.

READY TO APPLY?

If selected, you’ll be part of a meaningful, high-visibility event that celebrates food, community, and generosity.
